# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting and giving feedback professionally.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Harassment in public or private channels.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these
standards and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and
other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces
when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through the security
contact described in `SECURITY.md`.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
